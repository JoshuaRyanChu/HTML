FOOD AVENUE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 40% less fat graham wafer
  • 2 tsp. Kraft All Natural Peanut Butter
  • 1 Jet-Puffed Marshmallow, cut in half
  • 4 Baker's Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Spread wafer with peanut butter; place, peanut butter-side up, on microwaveable plate. Top with marshmallows, cut-sides up, and chocolate chips.
  2. Microwave on HIGH 25 sec. or until marshmallows puff and chocolate is melted.
  3. Serve immediately.
