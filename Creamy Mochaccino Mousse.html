FOOD AVENUE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1-1/2 cups cold milk
  • 1 pkg. (4-serving size) Jell-O Vanilla Instant Pudding
  • 1 Tbsp. Maxwell House Instant Coffee Original Roast
  • 1 cup thawed Cool Whip Whipped Topping
  • 3 oz. Baker's Semi-Sweet Chocolate, melted, cooled slightly

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Beat milk, pudding mix and coffee granules with whisk 2 min. Stir in Cool Whip.
  2. Spoon half evenly into 6 dessert dishes; drizzle with half the chocolate. Repeat layers.
